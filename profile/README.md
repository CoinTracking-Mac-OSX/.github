The platform handles detailed trade histories, real-time market updates, profit/loss summaries, and advanced tax reporting tools. Whether youâ€™re managing long-term investments or tracking high-frequency trades, CoinTracking provides a unified dashboard to oversee your entire crypto ecosystem.  

Users can import transactions automatically via API, CSV, or blockchain scanning, enabling seamless portfolio syncing across hundreds of services. The analytics engine processes trade volume, realized/unrealized gains, asset allocation, and historical performance in seconds.  

CoinTracking also includes powerful tax tools with support for FIFO, LIFO, HIFO, and many global tax rules. Its reporting system helps simplify compliance, generate tax forms, and reduce manual errors during filing season.
